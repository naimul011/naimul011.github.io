About

I am a Graduate Research Assistant at Alfred University, pursuing an M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering with a focus on artificial intelligence and machine learning. My research primarily explores catastrophic forgetting in Large Language Models (LLMs), computer vision applications, and natural language processing.

I am also the founder of Gradmate.ai, an AI platform that assists graduate students in finding programs and crafting application materials using advanced LLM orchestration techniques. My work has been published in several international conferences, and I have created multiple open-source datasets for the research community.

Recently, I achieved recognition by tying for 1st place in the 2024 Advizex AI Innovation Challenge, where I developed an AI sales bot projected to increase ROI by 25%.
