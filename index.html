About Me

Passionate about pushing the boundaries of AI and machine learning

Driving Innovation in AI Research

I'm a dedicated AI researcher currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering at Alfred University, specializing in artificial intelligence, computer vision, and natural language processing. My research focuses on understanding and mitigating catastrophic forgetting in Large Language Models.

With a strong background in teaching and research, I've published multiple papers in international conferences and have created several open-source datasets. I'm also the founder of Gradmate.ai, an AI platform that assists graduate students in their academic journey.

Recently, I tied for 1st place in the 2024 Advizex AI Innovation Challenge, developing an AI sales bot projected to increase ROI by 25%.
